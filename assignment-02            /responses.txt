What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
    An IP address is just a random set of numbers made specifically for that website, DNS is a system that looks up those numbers to give you a domain name instead of just a bunch of numbers.
What is a cookie?
The way a website remembers who you are. It is an ID card for the internet. Each time you log into a new website cookies will give a number that can be saved to always remember you.
